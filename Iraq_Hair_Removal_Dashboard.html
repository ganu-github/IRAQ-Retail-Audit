Key Strategic Insights

1. FEM is the Value Leader (27.8% share) but Cream-Dependent (99% of units)
FEM dominates Depilatory Creams (55.9% share, 1,457K units). However, FEM is completely absent from Paste (3,257K units, 37.6% of category)—the largest segment. This structural gap makes FEM vulnerable if category shifts toward pastes.
2. Premium Positioning Delivers Superior Economics
FEM's PPU (3,437 IQD/pack) is 1.6x the market average (2,089). This premium reflects selective distribution in Perfumery and strong brand equity. Distribution efficiency: 27.8% value share with only ~10% weighted distribution = 2.75x efficiency ratio (highest tier).
3. Growth Opportunities: +17% upside to 32.6% share (5.9 Bn IQD)
Three vectors: (1) Market consolidation in Perfumery (+2pp share = 360 Mn IQD), (2) Paste entry at 15% penetration (+165 Mn IQD), (3) Kurdish region expansion (+326 Mn IQD). Combined: +859 Mn IQD uplift.

Sharp Insights — Strategic View (using K Units / packs as the comparable basis)

1. Paste (Halawa) is the true volume leader, not Wax Strips
By consumer packs, Paste is 37.6% of the category, the largest segment by units. This was invisible in RetailZoom KGs view (which made Wax Strips appear 89% due to pack weight distortion). Wax Strips are actually only 4% by units. The critical insight: Paste (3.3 Bn IQD) is FEM's biggest white space.
2. FEM is a clear #1 in value, but under-indexed in volume diversity
FEM leads in Depilatory Creams (55.9% share, 1,457K units = 29.2% of total category). By value, FEM is #1 overall (27.8% share, 5,042 Bn IQD). But FEM is completely absent from Paste (37.6% of units), Powder (14.1%), and Spray (9.8%). Portfolio concentration risk is high.
3. Paste segment is fragmented & price-sensitive—massive entry opportunity
Paste (3,257K units, 3.3 Bn IQD) has no clear leader—top brand SOFT SKIN SADA is only 38.9%. This is structurally different from Creams (FEM 55.9%, concentrated) or Wax Strips (VEET 96.3%, monopolistic). Fragmentation + low concentration = high elasticity for new entrants with right pricing.
